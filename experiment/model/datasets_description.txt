First 1.5 GB from english wikipedia dump from Polyglot project: 
https://sites.google.com/site/rmyeid/projects/polyglot#TOC-Download-Wikipedia-Text-Dumps

Pre-processing with Matt Mahoney's Perl script (at the bottom of the page):
http://mattmahoney.net/dc/textdata.html
perl wikifil.pl enwik9 > text
Resulting text consists only of lowercase letters (a-z, converted from A-Z), and spaces (never consecutive).  
All other characters are converted to spaces. Digits are spelled out.
